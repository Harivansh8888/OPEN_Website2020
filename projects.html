MOBILE APP iNNOVATION CHALLENGE

Think outside of the box

The competition consist of two categories


Solve-It(Category-1 only):
 If you have an idea that satisfies any of the given theme, find a solution, map it into a prototype without code, and submit it. One team will be announced as a winner
Execute-It:
 Select a problem that satisfies any of the given theme, find a solution, convert it into a code and execute it on a mobile device. Three team will be selected as winners


The competition consist of two different categories


Category-1:
Students of higher education (including universities, colleges, polytechnics, undergraduate, or postgraduate degrees of all disciplines)
Category-2:
Students of Senior Secondary (11th and 12th Class Students).

Rewards & Appreciation:


Prizes worth ₹15,00,000 (Approx) will be rewarded to Winners and Runner-up teams along with certificates from OPEN community and will get an offer for internship/training in industry/university.

  • Courses worth ₹11,00,000 from Linux Foundations for Winners and Runner-up teams.
  • Prizes worth ₹1,00,000 for Winners and Runner-up teams.
  • Top 10 Teams from Category-2 will get training worth ₹3,00,000 & Certification from University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, Dehradun, UK, along with attractive prizes.


Eligibilty


  • To be eligible to participate in the Mobile App iNNOVATION Challenge (MiC), you must be a student of higher education (including universities, colleges, polytechnics, undergraduate, or postgraduate degrees of all disciplines)
  • For category-2 Students from schools are invited (high school or intermediate school)
  • Students may register as a teams of two to three. No more than three students are allowed to form a team.
  • Multiple teams can participate from same university/college.
  • An idea cannot be accepted in both the categories.


Event Themes


DIGITAL SOCIETY Sustainable growth in society

Design and deploy digital technologies to increase social welfare and achieve sustainable growth of society. It can help people save time and money or become more efficient.


Healthy "U"Human And Natural Ecosystems

Develop ideas those help students/ researchers to find areas, connect them with study partners/ groups/ funding agencies, manage class notes, or find tutors


Educational Excellence Learning by sharing

Develop interdisciplinary collaborations to improve well-being for humans and natural ecosystems. Your app might focus on nutrition, exercise, sleep, stress management, or other factors.
